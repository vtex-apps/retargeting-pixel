## About

This application provides a service to connect your store to the Retargeting.biz ads service, using tracking pixel events.
   
## Functionalities

- Permit Retargeting.biz to track pixel events

## How to set up

1. Install the app from VTEX App Store;
2. Add your Retargeting Pixel ID. 
3. Save your changes.

## Comments

- For the management of ads, you must have a Retargeting account.
